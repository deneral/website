About

Environments built
to invite the viewer
into a world.

I'm Paolo Internò, a fantasy landscape illustrator focused on the TCG and TTRPG space. My work centres on a single discipline: building places that feel like they exist — environments that carry atmosphere, narrative, and a sense of scale without a character in sight.

I work in a stylised, graphic approach with strong silhouettes and deliberate colour palettes — designed to read at card scale and hold up on a printed page.

I'm currently seeking my first commissions and open to working with independent publishers, small studios, and game designers who need environment art for their projects.

What I illustrate

Land Card Art Full-bleed landscapes with strong silhouettes and lighting built to read at 2" card size.
Sourcebook Spreads Full-page environment illustrations for RPG books, campaign settings, and world atlases.
Adventure Module Art Location illustrations — dungeons, wilderness, settlements — that set tone and orient players.
Box & Cover Art Hero landscapes for game boxes and Kickstarter campaigns, built to carry title treatment.
World Building Visuals Establishing art for new settings — helping define the visual identity of a world from the ground up.
